The core surface of Wuduh. Use `variant="swipe"` for the elevated question cards in the flow, `accentTop` to mark an investor-ready / highlighted card.

```jsx
<Card eyebrow="Market · Question 4" title="Who is your first customer?">
  Be specific — one clear segment beats three vague ones.
</Card>

<Card variant="swipe" interactive>…</Card>
```

Variants: `default` · `raised` · `swipe`. Padding: `md` · `lg` · `flush`.
